Rules & How it Works

Short version of league structure and match rules. Full rulebook coming soon.

Formats & Divisions

  • 2v2 (max roster 3) and 4v4 (max roster 6)
  • Regional divisions: US East, US West, Europe, Asia, Australia/NZ, South America, Africa
  • 8week regular season, 1 match per week (Tuesdays)
  • Promotion & relegation at season end

Match Flow (Best of 5)

  • Mapset: Hardpoint S&D Overload Hardpoint S&D
  • Map/MM mode picks simplified: away bans one map/mode, host bans one picks alternate (Away: HP1, Host: SND2, Away: OVR3, Host: HP4, Away: SND5)
  • Admins can spectate; lobbies are invite-only

Disconnect Rules

  • S&D / Overload: finish the current round, pause, resume next round on player return
  • Hardpoint: play out 3v4 unless player returns or both teams agree to replay

Late Penalties

  • Map 1: 15minute grace; after that, FF map 1
  • Map 2: additional 5 minutes; 20 minutes total FF map 2
  • Map 3+: additional 5 minutes; 25 minutes total FF match

Points & Tiebreakers

  • Win = 3 points, Loss = 0 points
  • Tiebreaker: map differential (wins losses)

Tournament Points

  • 1st: 21 pts 2nd: 15 pts 3rd: 9 pts 4th: 3 pts

Appeals

Open a ticket in our Discord with match details (teams, map, timestamp, issue). League admins will review and respond.

Submitting Match Results

  1. Go to App Team.
  2. At the top, youll see your newest unreported match.
  3. Select Win or Loss and choose the map score (e.g., 3-1). Add optional notes if needed.
  4. Submit your result. The other team must also submit their result.
  5. When both teams agree on the winner, the result is auto-confirmed and standings update (minor map-count differences are okay).
  6. If teams disagree on the winner, the match status becomes Needs Resolution and is reviewed via Discord ticket.
